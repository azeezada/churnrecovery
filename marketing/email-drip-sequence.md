- **Automation trigger:** Tag "waitlist" added

1. **Waitlist signup** → Add ConvertKit tag "waitlist"

3. **Product Hunt upvote** → Add tag "ph-supporter"
4. **Reply to email** → Forward to personal inbox for manual response
