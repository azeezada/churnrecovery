## 3 Article Concepts (Fully Outlined)

---

### Article 1: "The Hidden Cost of Subscriber Churn (And 3 Ways to Stop It Today)"

**Hook:**
Most subscription businesses track their churn rate. Almost none of them track the second-order cost — the lifetime value gap created when a churned subscriber doesn't come back. That gap compounds. And it's usually two to three times what founders expect.

**Outline:**
1. **What churn actually costs** — MRR loss is only the beginning; LTV reduction, CAC payback extension, and compounding effects
2. **The cancel flow blind spot** — most SaaS products treat cancellation as inevitable; the data shows 15–30% of cancellations are recoverable at the moment they happen
3. **Three interventions that actually work** — (a) personalized pause offers, (b) targeted retention offers by plan type, (c) exit surveys that create re-engagement triggers
4. **Why "just build a better product" isn't enough** — even beloved products lose subscribers to payment failures, life events, and friction
5. **The math: what one recovered subscriber is worth** — walk through a real calculation showing how small improvements compound at scale

**ChurnRecovery tie-in:** Position ChurnRecovery as the free, no-code way to implement all three interventions — designed for founders who don't have time to build this themselves.

---

### Article 2: "Why Your Cancel Button Is Your Most Valuable Real Estate"

**Hook:**
You've optimized your homepage. Your onboarding. Your pricing page. The one screen most subscription businesses have never touched is the one that appears when a subscriber decides to leave. That screen has a higher ROI than almost anything else you'll build this year.

**Outline:**
1. **The economics of the cancel moment** — a user who reaches the cancel screen has already decided; most products wave goodbye; the ones who intervene recover 20–40% of those users
2. **What bad cancel flows look like** — just a "cancel subscription" button, no friction, no value reminder, no alternative offered
3. **What great cancel flows do differently** — segment by user type, offer targeted alternatives (pause, downgrade, discount), ask why they're leaving
4. **Real examples of cancel flow copy that converts** — specific phrases, offer structures, and timing patterns that work
5. **How to build this without an engineering team** — no-code cancel flow tools vs. building custom; where ChurnRecovery fits

**ChurnRecovery tie-in:** Introduce ChurnRecovery as the tool that turns a cancel button into a retention moment — free, Stripe-native, 5-minute setup.

---

### Article 3: "Free vs. Paid Churn Tools: What Actually Moves the Needle for Small Businesses"

**Hook:**
Churnkey costs $250/month. ProfitWell Retain charges a percentage of recovered revenue. Baremetrics is another $129/month on top of that. If you're a bootstrapped SaaS or newsletter creator, you're paying more for churn tools than churn costs you. This post breaks down what actually matters — and what you can get for free.

**Outline:**
1. **The paid churn tool landscape** — what tools like Churnkey, ProfitWell Retain, and Brightback actually do and what they cost
2. **What you actually need vs. what you're paying for** — most small businesses don't need ML-powered churn prediction; they need a cancel flow and a failed payment email sequence
3. **The free tier reality check** — which tools have free plans that are genuinely useful vs. crippled freemium traps
4. **DIY approaches that work** — Stripe webhooks + basic email sequences vs. dedicated tools; when it's worth rolling your own
5. **Decision framework** — simple matrix: business size × churn rate × ARR → which tool tier makes sense

**ChurnRecovery tie-in:** ChurnRecovery is the free alternative for businesses that want enterprise cancel flow functionality without the enterprise price tag. Honest comparison with paid tools included.
