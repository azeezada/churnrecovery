## 4. Review Collection Strategy

### Goal: 5 reviews on G2 + 5 on Capterra within 30 days of going live

---

### Timing: When to Ask

| Trigger | Platform | Rationale |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| After first saved customer | G2 + Capterra | Peak satisfaction moment — they just saw ROI |
| After 30 days active | G2 first | Enough experience to write a substantive review |
| After 60 days active | Capterra | Capterra buyers weigh long-term reviews more |
| After recovering $500+ | Both | Clear, concrete success story to share |

**Rule:** Never ask for a review before the user has experienced a win. Ask after they've saved their first customer or recovered their first failed payment.

---

### What You Can Legally Offer as Incentives

**Allowed on G2 and Capterra:**
- ✅ Extended free trial (add 30–90 days of a paid tier)
- ✅ Feature unlock (grant access to a premium feature, e.g., A/B testing)
- ✅ Early access to upcoming features (beta access)
- ✅ Priority support for 90 days
- ✅ A shoutout/featured customer story on your blog or social media

**Not allowed:**
- ❌ Cash or gift cards in exchange for a positive review
- ❌ Conditional rewards ("we'll give you X if your review is 5 stars")
- ❌ Asking for positive reviews specifically — you can only ask for honest reviews

**Recommended incentive:**
> "Leave an honest review on G2 and we'll unlock 90 days of Growth tier free — that's A/B testing, unlimited subscribers, and priority support."

This is generous, compliant, and the "honest review" framing keeps it legal.
