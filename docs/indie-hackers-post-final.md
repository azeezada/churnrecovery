# How I Built a Free Alternative to Churnkey (and Why SaaS Tools Shouldn't Cost $825/Month)

*Posted to Indie Hackers — @dawoodazeeza*

---

Six months ago I was helping a newsletter creator friend set up churn recovery. She was losing subscribers every month to failed payments and cancellations — hundreds of dollars slipping away — but every tool I found to fix it wanted $250–$825/month.

For a bootstrapped creator doing $4k MRR, that's just absurd. It's like charging $300/month for a seatbelt.

So I built the alternative myself.

---

## Introducing ChurnRecovery

**ChurnRecovery** — everything Churnkey does, starting at $0.

🔗 **[churnrecovery.com](https://churnrecovery.com)**

It helps newsletter creators, coaches, online course sellers, and subscription businesses stop losing customers at the moment they're about to cancel. Smart cancel flows show the right offer (pause, discount, plan change) at cancellation. Failed payment recovery sends automated dunning emails to recover lapsed billing. Exit surveys tell you exactly why people are leaving.

Setup takes about 15 minutes. No developers needed.

## Why Free?

Hot take: churn recovery tools should be a commodity by now. The tech isn't revolutionary — it's cancel flow UI, email APIs, and webhook handling. What Churnkey charges $825/month for should cost maybe $25.

By making the core product free, we're betting on volume over margin. Get 10,000 users in the segment Churnkey ignores, build real data on what works, monetize the edges (advanced analytics, managed services, enterprise SLA). The ProfitWell playbook — free product as the wedge, premium on top.

But honestly? Even if the business model takes a while to figure out, this tool existing saves the community thousands in unnecessary SaaS fees. That's worth something.
