# ChurnRecovery — Product Hunt Launch Kit
*Target Launch Date: **Tuesday, April 7, 2026** at 12:01 AM PST*

> **Why April 7?** April 1 is April Fools' Day — serious product launches get confused for jokes and mocked. April 7 (Tuesday) is the optimal Product Hunt day with no conflicts. Tuesday is historically the highest-traffic PH day. See `docs/launch-timing-analysis.md`.

## 1. Tagline (60 chars max)

```
Stop losing subscribers. No dev team required.
```
*(47 chars)*

**Alternatives (in case A/B testing PH allows):**
- `Recover churning customers — free forever` (42 chars)
- `Cancel flows & dunning for $0/mo vs $825/mo` (44 chars)

**Why this works for business owners:** It speaks to the fear (losing subscribers), removes the objection (no technical expertise needed), and doesn't bury the lede with jargon like "churn recovery" or "dunning."

## 4. Maker Comment (First Comment)

Post this within the first 2 minutes of launch going live:

---

**Hey PH! 👋 Dawood here, founder of ChurnRecovery.**

Six months ago I was helping a newsletter creator friend set up churn recovery. She was losing subscribers every month to failed payments and cancellations — hundreds of dollars slipping away — but every tool I found to fix it wanted $250–$825/month. For a bootstrapped creator doing $4k MRR, that's just absurd. It's like charging $300/month for a seatbelt.

So I built ChurnRecovery: smart cancel flows that save customers at the moment they're about to leave, automated recovery emails for failed payments, and exit surveys that tell you *why* people are leaving. The whole thing takes about 15 minutes to set up — no developer needed, no code to write. And it's free. Not "free trial" — actually free, forever, for small teams.

If you're running a newsletter, online course, coaching program, or any subscription business, give it a shot. I'd love to hear what you think — I'm replying to every comment today. 🙏

**Try it:** [churnrecovery.com](https://churnrecovery.com)

---

**Tone notes:** Personal story (not corporate), names a real person helped, explains the pricing problem with a concrete analogy, ends with a direct invitation. Keep it human.

### 🎯 Hunt Request Strategy
Contact potential hunters by March 28th (4 days before). See Section 7 for the list.

**The ask email/DM (short):**
> Hi [Name], I'm launching ChurnRecovery on Product Hunt April 7th — it's a free churn recovery tool for subscription businesses (free alternative to Churnkey's $825/mo). Would you be open to hunting it? Happy to provide all assets, copy, and a personal walkthrough. The PH community you've built would be a perfect fit.

### 💬 Reply Strategy for Early Comments
- **Reply within 5 minutes** of any comment in the first 6 hours
- **Template responses to have ready:**
  - "Thanks for the love! 🙏 [Personalized follow-up question or detail]"
  - For "How does it work?" → brief 3-step answer, link to the GIF
  - For "What's the catch?" → "No catch — free forever for small teams, paid plans if you scale up. We're betting on volume over margin."
  - For competitors → "Churnkey is great for enterprise. We're built for bootstrappers and creators who can't justify $250+/month."
- **Don't argue, don't be defensive**
- **Ask questions back** — "What's your biggest churn challenge right now?" — keeps comment thread active
