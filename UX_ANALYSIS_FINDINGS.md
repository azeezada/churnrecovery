## Site Critique - ChurnRecovery.com

### ✅ Strong Points
1. **Clear value proposition** - Homepage immediately states "Your subscribers are leaving. Most would stay if you asked."
2. **Business-owner focused messaging** - Speaks to newsletter creators, coaches, course sellers specifically 
3. **Free positioning** - Prominently highlights "$0 per month, forever" vs competitors at $250-825/month
4. **Solid social proof** - Customer testimonials with specific names and use cases
5. **Complete feature coverage** - Cancel flows, payment recovery, analytics, brand customization
6. **Technical credibility** - 5-minute setup, one-line integration, works with Stripe/Paddle

### 🚨 Critical UX Issues

#### 1. **No Product Screenshots Above Fold**
- Hero section has NO visual proof of the actual product
- Potential customers can't see what they're signing up for
- Competitors show product interfaces prominently

#### 2. **Waitlist Friction**
- Main CTA leads to waitlist, not actual product trial
- No "see demo" or "try it now" option without email capture
- Creates unnecessary barrier for evaluation

#### 3. **Navigation Clarity Issues**
- "Demo" link unclear - is it interactive or just screenshots?
- "Get started free" vs "Join Waitlist" mixed messaging
- Missing clear "How it works" navigation item

#### 4. **Mobile Experience Gaps**
- Header navigation collapses but mobile menu not visible in HTML
- Desktop-optimized layouts may not translate well to mobile
- Need mobile-specific UX audit

#### 5. **Trust Signals Missing**
- No logos of actual customer companies
- No case studies with real metrics
- GitHub link present but not prominently featured as credibility signal
